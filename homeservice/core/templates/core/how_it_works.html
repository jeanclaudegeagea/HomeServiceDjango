Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

First create an account then simply browse through our services, select the one you need, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking through your account bookings.

How are service providers verified?

Service providers must submit professional licenses, certifications, and insurance documents which are verified by our team.

How do I become a service provider?

Register as a service provider, complete your profile with specializations and experience, upload required documents, and start listing your services.
